After Hours/Emergencies

A physician is always on call and available to help you with urgent and emergency problems. After office hours, holidays, and weekends, you may call our office at (205) 787-8676, and our answering service will alert the doctor on call.  Prescription and non-urgent problems should be handled during routine business hours.

Making Appointments

Appointments are scheduled for Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays at Princeton Baptist Medical Center during regular office hours.  For your convenience, Dr. Cezayirli also sees patients at UAB Medical Center West on Monday mornings. To make an appointment with a physician, please call our office staff at (205) 787-8676 during regular office hours.

Staff

The Office Staff at Haynes Neurosurgical Group is here during regular office hours to assist you with your medical needs and inquiries. They also facilitate scheduling tests, surgery, and other procedures; acquire necessary pre-authorizations; complete employer paperwork; and file insurance claims.

In order to reach a member of our staff, simply call the office at (205) 787-8676 and your call will be transferred to the appropriate person.
